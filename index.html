About me

My name is Sheng Yonghao. I received my Bachelor of Engineering degree in Electronic and Information Engineering from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in June 2025. I am currently enrolled as a Ph.D. candidate at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences (UCAS). My research focuses on foundational artificial intelligence, with specific interests in Game AI, Scientific Formula Discovery (Symbolic Regression), and LLM-based Agents.
